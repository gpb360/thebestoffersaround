## Purpose

This site is an affiliate marketing website that:
1. Reviews men's health supplements (testosterone boosters, male enhancement pills, HGH releasers, nootropics)
2. Provides educational content about men's health topics
3. Compares products based on ingredients, clinical research, and user reviews
4. Earns affiliate commissions when visitors purchase recommended products

**Target Audience:** Men aged 30-65 looking for science-backed information about supplements for:
- Low testosterone
- Erectile dysfunction
- Anti-aging (HGH)
- Weight loss
- Cognitive enhancement

## SEO Strategy

### Keyword Strategy
- **Primary:** "best testosterone booster", "male enhancement pills", "natural ED remedies"
- **Secondary:** Product-specific keywords ("Testosil review", "VigRX Plus vs Erectin")
- **Long-tail:** Question keywords ("Does ashwagandha increase testosterone?", "What is the best male enhancement pill?")
- **LSI:** Related terms (KSM-66, L-Arginine, fenugreek, D-Aspartic Acid)

### On-Page SEO
- Schema markup (Article, Product, Organization)
- Open Graph tags for social sharing
- Twitter Cards
- Proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- Internal linking between related posts
- Image alt text optimization
- Meta descriptions (150-160 characters)

### Technical SEO
- Sitemap: `/sitemap-index.xml` (includes all blog posts and products)
- Robots.txt: `/robots.txt` (allows all crawlers)
- RSS feeds: `/rss.xml` and `/category/[category].xml`
- Fast loading (Astro static/server hybrid)
- Mobile-responsive (Tailwind CSS)
- HTTPS (Vercel hosting)

## Content Strategy

### Tone & Voice
- **Authoritative but accessible** - Science-backed but easy to understand
- **Objective** - Balanced reviews highlighting pros and cons
- **Action-oriented** - Clear CTAs and next steps
- **Empathetic** - Understanding of sensitive men's health issues

### Content Pillars
1. **Education** - How supplements work, ingredient science
2. **Reviews** - Honest product assessments
3. **Comparisons** - Head-to-head product comparisons
4. **Guides** - How-to content for specific goals
5. **FAQ** - Common questions answered

### Writing Guidelines
- Cite clinical studies when available
- Include dosage information
- Mention potential side effects
- Provide realistic expectations
- Link to scientific sources (PubMed, journals)
- Update content annually for accuracy
