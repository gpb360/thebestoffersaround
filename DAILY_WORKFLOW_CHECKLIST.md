**Project:** The Best Offers Around
**Daily Commitment:** 90 minutes
**Goal:** 1,000 visitors in 30 days

## How to Use This Guide

### Print This Checklist
- Print 30 copies (one for each day)
- Check off tasks as completed
- Track time spent on each section
- Note results and observations

- **Morning (30 min):** Content creation + scheduling

## Daily Mindset Reminders

### Remember These Principles
1. **Value First, Self-Promo Later** - 90/10 rule
2. **Consistency Wins** - Show up every day
3. **Engage Authentically** - Real conversations, not bots
4. **Track Everything** - Data shows what works
5. **Adjust Quickly** - Double down on wins, cut losses
6. **Be Patient** - Traffic compounds over time
7. **Help People** - Karma (both Reddit and real) pays off

### Daily Affirmation
```
"I am building a valuable resource that helps men make informed health
decisions. Every piece of content I create, every conversation I have,
and every connection I make brings me closer to my goal of 1,000 visitors
in 30 days. I focus on providing value, and the traffic will follow."
```
