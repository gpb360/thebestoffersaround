## üìã **CONTENT STRATEGY BY PLATFORM**

### **Facebook Strategy**
- **Post Frequency:** 1-2x daily
- **Content Types:**
  - Blog post shares (60%)
  - Product spotlights (20%)
  - Health tips (10%)
  - Engagement questions (10%)
- **Best Times:** 9am, 3pm, 7pm EST
- **Format:** Long-form posts with images, link previews

### **Instagram Strategy**
- **Post Frequency:**
  - Feed posts: 1x daily
  - Stories: 3-5x daily
  - Reels: 2-3x weekly
- **Content Types:**
  - Product photos (30%)
  - Infographics (25%)
  - Before/after (20%)
  - Lifestyle/benefits (15%)
  - Stories: Polls, Q&A, behind scenes (10%)
- **Best Times:** 11am, 7pm EST
- **Format:** Square (1080x1080), Portrait (1080x1350), Stories (1080x1920)

### **Twitter/X Strategy**
- **Post Frequency:** 3-5x daily
- **Content Types:**
  - Blog links (40%)
  - Quick tips (30%)
  - Engagement tweets (20%)
  - Retweets/replies (10%)
- **Best Times:** 8am, 12pm, 6pm EST
- **Format:** Short, hashtags, threads

### **LinkedIn Strategy**
- **Post Frequency:** 1x daily (weekdays)
- **Content Types:**
  - Professional insights (50%)
  - Research findings (30%)
  - Product reviews (20%)
- **Best Times:** 8am, 5pm EST
- **Format:** Professional, longer-form

### **Pinterest Strategy**
- **Post Frequency:** 5-10x daily (pins)
- **Content Types:**
  - Infographics (40%)
  - Product images (30%)
  - Blog posts (20%)
  - How-to guides (10%)
- **Format:** Vertical images (1000x1500)
