## âš¡ Quick Tips for Better Graphics

### If a graphic doesn't look right:
1. **Regenerate** - KIE.AI creates variations
2. **Adjust prompt** - Remove or add elements
3. **Edit in Canva** - Fix text or colors manually

### Common Issues:
- **Text cut off:** Regenerate with shorter text
- **Colors wrong:** Add "dark background with gold accents" to prompt
- **Image blurry:** Increase resolution or regenerate
- **Not enough detail:** Add "professional, detailed" to prompt
