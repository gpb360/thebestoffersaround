## Why Pinterest? (The Untapped Goldmine)

- **Long-tail traffic:** Pins show in search for months/years

- **High CTR:** Men's health pins get 2-3x higher click-through

## Pin Creation Strategy

### Pin Dimensions & Specs

#### Standard Pin (Recommended)
- **Dimensions:** 1000x1500px (2:3 ratio)
- **Max size:** 10MB
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Text:** Headline + 3-5 bullet points

#### Long Pin (For listicles)
- **Dimensions:** 1000x2000px (1:2 ratio)
- **Best for:** "10 Foods That Kill Testosterone" style
- **Note:** Can get cut off on mobile - test carefully

#### Square Pin (Not recommended)
- **Dimensions:** 1000x1000px
- **Issue:** Takes less screen space, lower CTR
