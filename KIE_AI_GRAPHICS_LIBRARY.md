## Platform Specifications

### Instagram Feed Posts
- **Dimensions:** 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1350px (portrait)
- **Style:** Bold, eye-catching, text-heavy
- **Colors:** Gold (#D4AF37), dark gray (#1A1A1A), black (#0A0A0A)
- **Font:** Sans-serif, bold, easy to read

### Instagram Stories
- **Dimensions:** 1080x1920px (9:16 vertical)
- **Style:** Minimal, focus on key message
- **Text:** Large, clear, limited words

### Pinterest Pins
- **Dimensions:** 1000x1500px (2:3 vertical)
- **Style:** Informative, list-based, educational
- **Text:** Headline + 3-5 bullet points

### Facebook/Twitter
- **Dimensions:** 1200x630px (landscape)
- **Style:** Professional, informative
- **Text:** Moderate, clear hierarchy

### LinkedIn
- **Dimensions:** 1200x627px (landscape)
- **Style:** Professional, clean, business-appropriate
- **Text:** Data-driven, charts, statistics

## Tips for Better Graphics

### Do's ‚úÖ
- Use consistent color scheme (gold + dark)
- Keep text minimal and readable
- Use bold, clear fonts
- Leave white/negative space
- Focus on one message per graphic
- Test variations (A/B test)

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- Don't overcrowd with text
- Don't use low contrast colors
- Don't use more than 2-3 fonts
- Don't ignore platform specs
- Don't use copyrighted images
- Don't sacrifice readability for style

---

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Instagram
- Use bold, eye-catching designs
- Test both square and portrait
- Save stories highlights
- Use consistent filter/style

### Pinterest
- Vertical format only (2:3 ratio)
- Text-overlay is critical
- Educational content performs best
- Create multiple pins per blog post

### Facebook
- Testimonials work well
- Comparison graphics get engagement
- Keep it professional but approachable
- Use carousel posts for multiple points

### Twitter
- Keep text minimal
- Focus on one stat or fact
- Bold, simple designs
- Thread starters need to be eye-catching

### LinkedIn
- Data-driven designs
- Professional aesthetic
- Charts and graphs
- Research-backed visuals
