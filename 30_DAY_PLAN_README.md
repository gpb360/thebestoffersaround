## üéì Key Principles

### 1. Value First, Self-Promotion Later
**90% value, 10% promotion**

- Provide helpful information
- Answer questions thoroughly
- Share personal experiences
- Only link when genuinely relevant

### 2. Consistency Wins
**Show up every day for 90 minutes**

- Post at the same times daily
- Engage in communities regularly
- Build momentum over time
- Don't skip days

### 3. Engage Authentically
**Real conversations, not bots**

- Be helpful, not promotional
- Admit when you don't know
- Share failures and successes
- Build genuine connections

### 4. Track Everything
**Data shows what works**

- Monitor PostHog analytics
- Track social media metrics
- Note what content performs best
- Double down on wins, cut losses

### 5. Be Patient
**Traffic compounds over time**

- Week 1-2 will be slow
- Week 3-4 breakthrough happens
- Momentum builds gradually
- Long-term mindset

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### Don't Do This ‚ùå

1. **Spamming links** everywhere immediately
2. **Ignoring Reddit rules** (you'll get banned)
3. **Inconsistent posting** (random times/days)
4. **Being overly promotional** (sell, sell, sell)
5. **Giving up too early** (Week 1 is slowest)
6. **Ignoring analytics** (flying blind)
7. **Burnout from overwork** (stick to 90 min)

### Do This Instead ‚úÖ

1. **Build trust first** (link later)
2. **Read subreddit rules** before posting
3. **Post consistently** (same times daily)
4. **Provide massive value** (help, don't sell)
5. **Stay consistent** for 30 days minimum
6. **Track everything** and adjust
7. **Sustainable pace** (90 min is enough)
